Gari and Beans

Recipe for Gari and Beans

Hello there! Today I will be teaching you how to make gari and beans.

Ingredients

  • Black eyed peas
  • Palm oil
  • Tomatoes (2)
  • Onion (1)
  • Powdered pepper
  • Salt
  • Ripe plantain
  • Cooking oil
  • Gari (Ground cassava)

Steps

  1. Soak 2 cups of black eyed peas in water overnight
  2. Filter water from black eyed peas
  3. Add 7 cups of water and 7 pinches of salt
  4. Cook until boiling and lower heat
  5. Cook until black eyed peas are soften (About 45 mins)
  6. Take black eyed peas of heat afterwards
  7. Slice up an onion and a tomato and fry in palm oil
  8. Add a table spoon of powdered chilli and stir until palm oil starts to darken
  9. Put off heat
  10. Slice up ripe plantains and fry using cooking oil until it turns brown
  11. Serve beans with gari, palm oil sauce and fried plantain
  12. Enjoy
