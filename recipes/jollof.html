Jollof

Recipe for making Jollof rice

Hello there! Today you will learn how to make jollof rice. This recipe is based on the Ghanaian version of the meal, and is very simple to follow.

Ingredients

  • Large red bell pepper (1)
  • Vine tomatoes (2)
  • Red onion (1)
  • Red scotch bonnet chillies (2)
  • Garlic cloves (3)
  • Fresh ginger (25g)
  • Vegetable oil (150 ml)
  • Rice (2 cups)
  • Chicken stock cubes (3)
  • Water (600 ml)

Steps

  1. Blend pepper, tomatoes, onions, chillies, garlic cloves, and fresh ginger.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a sauce pan on medium heat.
  3. Add onion and stir fry for 3 minutes then add blended ingredients from step 1.
  4. Cook for about 10 minutes and add chicken stock cubes.
  5. Rinse rice, drain and add to sauce pan.
  6. Allow to cook on low heat for 25 minutes.
  7. Serve with grilled chicken, fried plantain or both!
