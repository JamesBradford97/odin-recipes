Penne Pesto Pasta

Recipe for Penne Pesto Pasta

Hello there! Today I will be introducing my penne pasta recipe.

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta (500 g)
  • Pesto basil (190 g)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Measure 3 Litres of water in a sauce pan and add penne pasta
  2. Add 5 pinches of salt
  3. Cook pasta until boiling, then lower heat and cook until soft.
  4. Drain water off cooked pasta
  5. Add pesto basil to pasta and stir until mixed.
  6. Serve and enjoy
